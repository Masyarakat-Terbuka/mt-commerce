This page is a draft terms of service bundled with mt-commerce. Store operators are responsible for reviewing, adjusting, and publishing their own terms in line with the laws of their jurisdiction.

## Relationship between parties

mt-commerce is open-source software. A store running on this platform is operated by the store operator, not by the mt-commerce project. Purchases are made directly between the buyer and the store.

## Accounts

Buyers can create an account to save addresses, view order history, and speed up future checkouts. The account holder is responsible for keeping the password confidential and for activity carried out under the account.

## Orders

An order is binding once payment is received or confirmed by the operator. Before that, an order can be cancelled by the buyer or declined by the store, for example due to stock running out or a pricing error.

Prices and availability are shown based on the latest data the store has. Errors can occur; the operator may cancel an order with a pricing error before it ships.

## Payment

Payment is processed through the methods offered by the store. See the [Payment](/en/help/payment) page for an overview of supported methods.

## Shipping and returns

Shipping details and return policies are set by each store. See the [Shipping](/en/help/shipping) and [Returns](/en/help/returns) pages for an overview.

## Store-provided content

Store operators are responsible for the accuracy of product descriptions, images, prices, and marketing claims. mt-commerce as a platform does not review the content of each store.

## Limitation of liability

mt-commerce as software is provided as is, without warranty of fitness for a particular purpose. Store operators are responsible for the operation of their store, including order fulfilment, customer service, and legal compliance.

## Governing law

Disputes between buyers and stores are resolved under the law of the jurisdiction where the store operates. Operators state the relevant jurisdiction in their own terms of service.

## Changes to these terms

The terms of service can change over time. The "last updated" date appears at the top of this page.
