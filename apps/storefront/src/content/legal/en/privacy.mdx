This page is a draft privacy policy bundled with mt-commerce. Store operators are responsible for reviewing, adjusting, and publishing their own policy in line with the laws of their jurisdiction.

## Data collected

mt-commerce collects the following data as part of operating as a commerce platform:

- Name, email, and phone number entered when creating an account or placing an order
- Shipping and billing addresses
- Order and transaction history
- Basic technical data (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for security and diagnostics

Card data is not stored by mt-commerce. For gateway payments, card data is handled entirely by the gateway provider.

## How data is used

Collected data is used to:

- Process orders and ship goods
- Send order-related notifications (confirmation, shipping, returns)
- Support customer questions and claims
- Meet legal and tax obligations
- Maintain platform security and integrity

Data is not sold to third parties. Sharing happens only for direct operational needs — couriers for shipping, gateways for payment, authorities for legal obligations.

## Data retention

Data is kept while the account is active or for as long as needed to meet legal obligations (for example, tax records). Buyers can request deletion by contacting the store operator.

## Buyer rights

In line with general data-protection principles, buyers have the right to:

- Access personal data held about them
- Update inaccurate data
- Request deletion, subject to legal obligations
- Withdraw consent for processing, where relevant

Requests are made by contacting the store operator.

## Cookies and similar technologies

mt-commerce uses cookies to keep the login session, remember the cart, and preserve language preferences. Additional analytics cookies may be enabled by the operator as needed.

## Changes to this policy

The privacy policy can change over time. The "last updated" date appears at the top of this page.
