Every store running on mt-commerce sets up its own shipping. This page describes how shipping works in general, so buyers and store operators share the same picture.

## Supported couriers

mt-commerce connects to several Indonesian couriers through shipping integrations. The couriers available in a given store are decided by the operator, so the options at checkout can differ from one store to another.

Couriers commonly supported:

- JNE (REG, OKE, YES)
- J&T Express
- SiCepat
- AnterAja
- Pos Indonesia

In addition to inter-city shipping, some stores enable instant delivery through app-based couriers like GoSend and GrabExpress for in-city orders.

## Delivery estimates

When you pick a shipping method, the system shows a delivery estimate that comes directly from the courier. The estimate is not a promise — most parcels arrive on time, but weather, holidays, and courier warehouse conditions can affect the actual time.

Rough ranges:

- In-city: 1–2 business days
- Java island: 2–4 business days
- Outside Java: 3–7 business days
- Remote areas: 5–10 business days

## Shipping cost

Shipping cost is calculated automatically based on destination, parcel weight, and the chosen courier. The total appears before payment, so there are no surprise fees after the order is placed.

## Order tracking

After an order ships, the courier's tracking number is shown on the account page and sent by email. Tracking can be done directly on the courier's site.

## Order-specific questions

For questions about a specific order — late parcel, wrong address, claims — contact the store. Each store handles its own customer support, with contact details usually shown on the contact page or in the order confirmation email.
