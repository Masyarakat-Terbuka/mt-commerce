mt-commerce supports several payment methods commonly used in Indonesia. Which methods are available in a given store is decided by the operator.

## Manual bank transfer

The buyer chooses a destination bank at checkout, then transfers the amount based on instructions emailed after the order is created. The store operator confirms payment manually after funds arrive.

This is a fit for stores that do not yet need a paid gateway, or that handle volumes manageable by hand. There are no transaction fees from a third party.

## Payment gateway (Midtrans)

For stores that want automatic payment confirmation, mt-commerce integrates with Midtrans. Through Midtrans, buyers can pay with:

- Virtual Accounts (BCA, BRI, BNI, Mandiri, Permata)
- E-wallets (GoPay, ShopeePay, OVO, Dana)
- QRIS
- Credit and debit cards
- Retail outlets (Indomaret, Alfamart)

Successful payments automatically move the order to "Paid" without manual confirmation.

## Cash on delivery (COD)

For deliveries through couriers that support it, mt-commerce enables cash on delivery via the Biteship integration. Buyers pay cash to the courier when the parcel arrives.

COD is usually limited to certain regions and order values, per courier rules. Operators can disable COD if they prefer.

## Security

mt-commerce does not store buyer card data. For gateway payments, card data is handled entirely by the PCI-DSS-compliant gateway provider. For manual bank transfer, mt-commerce only stores the transfer reference entered by the operator.
