These are common questions about the mt-commerce platform itself. For questions about a specific order, contact the store you bought from.

## What is mt-commerce?

mt-commerce is an open-source commerce platform built for Indonesia. Store operators use it to run their online store — product catalog, cart, payments, shipping — without depending on paid foreign services.

## Is mt-commerce free to use?

Yes. The source code is on GitHub under the MIT license. Anyone can run, modify, or build on top of it without licensing fees.

## Am I buying from mt-commerce directly?

No. mt-commerce is software that store operators use to build their own stores. Your purchase is made directly with the store running the platform, not with the mt-commerce project.

## How do I contact a store?

Each store handles its own customer support. Contact details are usually shown on the store's contact page or in the footer, and in the order confirmation email.

## Is buyer data safe?

mt-commerce follows common data security practices — encrypted connections (HTTPS), no card data storage, session-based authorization. Practical responsibility for buyer data lies with the operator who runs the installation.

## Can I contribute to the project?

Yes. Code, issues, and discussions are open on [GitHub](https://github.com/masyarakat-terbuka/mt-commerce). Contributions in code, documentation, translations, or bug reports are all welcome.

## Does mt-commerce support English?

Yes. The interface and documentation are available in Indonesian and English. Purchases can be made in whichever language the store operator enables.
